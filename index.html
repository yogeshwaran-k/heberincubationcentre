About Us

Centre for Incubation and Innovation – Institution Innovation Cell – CII-IIC of Bishop Heber College functions with the objective of promoting innovative ideas, fostering creative expressions and enhancing the employable and entrepreneurial prospects of the students.

  • VISION
  • MISSION

OUR VISION

To be dynamic and visionary hub that cultivates innovation,entrepreneurship and collabaration,shaping the future of students and enhancing transformative ideas and ventures

OUR MISSION

Our mission is to nurture a culture of innovation and entrepreneurship among students providing them with the resources,mentorship and networking opportunities needed to ideate ,create and launch successfull start-ups.

Being a catalyst for innovation by fostering a culture of creativity and forward thinking solutions.

Startup Mentorship

Our incubation center offers tailored mentorship by industry experts, helping entrepreneurs refine ideas, develop strategies, and overcome challenges effectively.

Resourceful Workspace

We provide cutting-edge facilities and well-equipped workspaces that nurture innovation, offering startups a collaborative environment to ideate, collaborate, and realize their visions effectively.

Business Support

Our specialists aid startups in creating full business plans & market analyses. Through workshops, training, and tools, we empower startups to refine their business skills effectively.

Networking Opportunities

We arrange frequent networking events, workshops, and industry meet-ups, facilitating interactions between startups, like-minded entrepreneurs, leaders, and potential collaborators, cultivating vital connections to fuel growth.

Market Validation Support

We provide direction in performing market research and validation activities. By assisting startups in gathering customer insights, analyzing trends, and adapting offerings, we enable effective responses to market demands.

Access to Funding

Linking startups with investors and aiding in funding access is a core service. Through our network of angels, venture capitalists, and funding avenues, we enable startups to secure crucial capital for growth.
